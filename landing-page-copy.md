## SOLUTION SECTION

**Section Header:**
One workspace. Three systems. Built for developers.

---

### Template 1: Tech Interview Tracker
**Free with every download**

Stop losing track of where you are in each company's process.

- **LeetCode Kanban** — Organize problems by difficulty (Easy / Medium / Hard) and track which ones you've actually reviewed, not just solved once and forgotten. The "Needs Review" column is where real interview prep happens.
- **Company Application Pipeline** — Follow every application from "Applied" to "Offer" with built-in status stages. Never forget to send that follow-up email again.
- **Problem Notes DB** — Log your approach, time complexity, and schedule a spaced-repetition review date for each problem. Stop re-solving the same problem from scratch three months later.
- **Per-Company Interview Checklist** — Before every technical interview, run through a checklist tailored to the specific company. Walk in prepared, not anxious.

---

### Template 2: Side Project Dashboard
**Included in the paid bundle**

Treat your side project like a real product. Because it is.

- **Sprint Kanban** — Break your project into weekly sprints with a proper backlog. Stop working on random features and start shipping.
- **Pre-Deploy Checklist** — Security, performance, SEO, analytics — a launch checklist that covers what solo developers always forget under pressure.
- **Feature Roadmap** — Move ideas from "shower thought" to "shipped" with a visual roadmap scored by impact vs effort. Keep scope creep under control.
- **Bug Tracker** — Log bugs with severity labels so you're fixing the right things first, not the most annoying ones.

---

### Template 3: Developer Learning Log
**Included in the paid bundle**

You're learning constantly. Start actually retaining it.

- **Book Notes DB** — Capture key insights chapter by chapter. Add action items while the ideas are fresh, not two weeks after you finished the book.
- **Weekly Retrospective** — A structured template to review what went well, what didn't, and what you're focusing on next week. Takes 15 minutes. Worth every minute.
- **TIL Log** — Today I Learned. One entry per day. In six months, you'll have a searchable record of everything you've picked up.
- **Resource Library** — Bookmark articles, courses, and videos directly in Notion with tags and completion status. No more "I know I saved that somewhere."
