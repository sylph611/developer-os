## TWEET 4/9 — The Insight Behind the Tracker

Most people track problems they've solved.

I track problems I've solved AND when I need to re-solve them.

There's a difference.

If I solved a hard DP problem in week 1 and don't touch it until my interview in week 8, I will blank on it completely.

Scheduling a cold review 2 weeks later is the part nobody does. It's also the part that actually works.
